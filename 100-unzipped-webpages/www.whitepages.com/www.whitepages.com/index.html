What is Whitepages?

Whitepages is the authority in people search, established in 1997. With comprehensive contact information, including cell phone numbers, for over 275 million people nationwide, and SmartCheck, the fast, comprehensive background check compiled from criminal and other records from all 50 states. Landlords use Whitepages TenantCheck, which is designed expressly for screening rental applications. Whitepages provides answers to over 2 million searches every day and powers the top ranked domains: Whitepages, 411, and Switchboard.
